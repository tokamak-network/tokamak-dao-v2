GOVERNANCE
Delegation & Voting
📋
DelegateRegistry
"Voter Registry"
• vTON holders cannot vote directly
• Must delegate to a Delegate
• Anyone can register as Delegate
• More delegation = more power
• Re-delegation supported
🏛️
DAOGovernor
"Legislature"
• Proposal: burn 10 TON + 0.25%
• Voting period: 7 days
• Quorum: 4% of delegated vTON
• Pass: >50% simple majority
• Execution: 7-day Timelock wait
SAFETY MECHANISMS
Timelock & SecurityCouncil
⏱️
Timelock
"7-day safety buffer"
• Minimum 7-day execution delay
• Up to 30 days configurable
• 14-day grace period
• Owner of ALL V2 contracts
• Only Governor + SC can access

Even if a malicious proposal passes,
the community has 7 days to respond.
🛡️
SecurityCouncil
"Emergency override"
• 3 members (1 foundation + 2 external)
• 2/3 approval required (67%)
• No 7-day wait in emergencies

Can:
• Cancel malicious proposals
• Pause / resume protocol
• Emergency upgrades
• Custom actions
